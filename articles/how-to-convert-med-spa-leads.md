# How to Convert More Med Spa Leads (5x Framework)

## A direct framework for turning more inquiries into booked, showed, paid consults

Most med spa lead problems are not lead volume problems. They are conversion problems. Inquiries arrive through Google, Instagram, referrals, walk-ins, and the website, then leak out of the funnel because the practice cannot answer fast enough, follow up consistently enough, or close on the consult itself. Lift My Spa clients see up to 5x more leads converted by combining the AI front desk with structured follow-up. This is the framework.

## Step 1: Define the funnel honestly

Before you can fix conversion, you have to see the funnel. For a med spa, it looks like this.

1. Inquiry. Call, web form, chat, DM, walk-in.
2. Connection. Did a real conversation happen, in any channel?
3. Consult booked. Slot held on the calendar.
4. Consult showed. Client actually arrives.
5. Treatment booked. First treatment scheduled.
6. Treatment showed and paid.
7. Repeat treatment or membership.

Most practices lose half or more of their leads between steps 1 and 2. Then half of what is left between 3 and 4. Then more between 5 and 6. The 5x lift comes from plugging each leak.

## Step 2: Answer every inquiry within 60 seconds

The single largest leak is connection time. Industry-wide, over 50% of med spa calls go unanswered. Web forms get a reply hours later. DMs sit overnight.

Inquiries decay fast. A lead that calls three clinics in a row converts with the one that picks up first. The 60-second standard is non-negotiable.

The Lift My Spa AI front desk answers 100% of calls, web chats, and social DMs 24/7, in English and Spanish, and books on the spot. This single change usually doubles conversion before any other step is fixed.

## Step 3: Book the consult on first contact

Do not "send information and follow up". That is a lost lead. The right behavior is to hold the slot on the first conversation. Whether the conversation happens with the AI front desk, with staff, or with the website chat, the call to action is the booked consult, not the brochure.

## Step 4: Confirm and remind aggressively

Booked is not showed. The reminder cadence that works.

- Immediate confirmation at booking
- 7 days out for high-ticket consults
- 48 hours out
- 24 hours out
- Morning of, with prep and address

This sequence is one of the 15 pre-built Lift My Spa workflows. It uses the 35 SMS templates and 45 email templates already written for med spa voice.

## Step 5: Recover no-shows the moment they happen

When a consult misses, do not wait. Lift My Spa runs a no-show recovery sequence that texts within minutes, offers one-tap rescheduling, and follows up at 24 and 72 hours. A meaningful share of misses rebook when contacted immediately.

This is how Lift My Spa drives up to 50% fewer no-shows.

## Step 6: Run a real nurture sequence for slow leads

Not every lead books on day one. The middle 40% need nurturing. The right sequence runs for several weeks, with relevant content, social proof, before-and-after results, FAQs, and limited offers spaced correctly.

Manual nurture does not happen. Automated nurture does. Lift My Spa runs new lead nurture as a pre-built workflow.

## Step 7: Convert the consult to a treatment

The consult itself is a sales conversation. The provider needs to.

- Identify the right treatment plan
- Quote a clear next step
- Offer a package or membership when appropriate
- Book the treatment before the client leaves

If the consult ends with "we will call you", conversion drops. The platform supports the consult by surfacing client history, prior interactions, language preference, and previously expressed interests.

## Step 8: Reactivate dormant leads

A meaningful share of leads who never converted will book later. Reactivation campaigns to dormant leads are one of the highest ROI activities in the practice. Lift My Spa runs this as a pre-built workflow.

## Step 9: Cover Spanish-speaking leads in their language

In Texas, Florida, and Arizona, ignoring Spanish is leaving conversion on the table. Lift My Spa runs every channel and every workflow in both English and Spanish.

## Step 10: Measure the funnel weekly

Pull these from the Lift My Spa ROI dashboard.

- Inquiry-to-connection rate (target: 100%)
- Connection-to-booked-consult rate
- Booked-to-showed rate
- Show-to-treatment rate

Move whichever number is lowest. The 5x lift comes from compounding small improvements at every step, not one big change.
