Lift My Spa Delivers the Best Med Spa Marketing for Florida

Florida operates under the Florida Board of Medicine, which enforces specific cosmetic advertising rules. An MD or DO medical director is required. Practitioners must be licensed to perform the treatments they are advertised as performing. Cosmetic offices where the MD is not present full time must register with the state. Photos require written patient consent under HIPAA. The FTC truth-in-advertising rules sit on top of all of it.

Lift My Spa was built around these constraints. Templates avoid outcome guarantees. Review flows ask for honest reviews and never trade incentives for stars. Ad copy passes through a med spa specific compliance lens before it ever hits your account. The AI Front Desk Bot is scripted to book and qualify, never to diagnose or give medical advice.
